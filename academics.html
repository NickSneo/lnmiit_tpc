Academic Momentum


At LNMIIT, students work hard to create a bright future for themselves and for the community. They study various subjects and sharpen their intellectual and work-related skills from basic English to managing complex computer networks to critical thinking of analytical problems. This rigorous process empowers them to pursue their interests in the academia or in industry. To this end, the institute and students have taken several initiatives, a few of which are listed below:

Summer Trainings

1. Every year the institute offers an Industrial training to about 25 meritorious students from the 3rd year, at ArcelorMittal Steel Plant, Kazakhstan. The training is aimed at providing the students with an exposure to the Industrial environment at a global level.
2. Students go to various research labs and universities worldwide for summer projects. These include University of Manitoba, Canada; UAB, Spain; Western Michigan University, USA; INRIA, France; University of Cassino, Italy; ICCL Lab, CPU, Taiwan; Polytechnic University, New York, USA; University Of Warwick, UK; CBIA, Brno, Czech Republic; SUT, Thailand.
3. At LNMIIT, we facilitate student exchange programmes, under which students get an opportunity to spend a semester at elite institutions like Knoesis Centre, Ohio State University, USA; IIIT Hyderabad, IIT Gandhinagar and IIT Delhi.
4. Students have also done their summer training at renowned companies including TCS, CISCO, Microsoft, Ericsson etc.
5. Students get a chance to receive training at CERN, Switzerland, which is one of the world's largest laboratories founded by the European union. It is also the birthplace of World Wide Web (WWW).
Summer projects are also undertaken by the students at elite institutes including IITs, IISc Bangalore, IIIT Hyderabad, IIIT Delhi and BITS Pilani.
